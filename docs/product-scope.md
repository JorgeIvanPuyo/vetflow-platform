
# Product Scope

## Product Name
Vetflow Platform

## Product Type
Multi-tenant veterinary clinical management platform.

## Initial Goal
Build a functional MVP for real pilot usage by 3 veterinary users. The purpose of this phase is to validate the clinical workflow, gather feedback, and evolve the product into a monetizable SaaS solution.

## Core Business Problem
Veterinary professionals need a structured and accessible way to manage:
- patient information
- owner data
- consultation records
- exams and results
- case traceability
- fast access to clinical history

## MVP Priorities
The veterinary lead prioritized the following modules as critical:

### High Priority
1. Patient and owner management
2. Clinical history and consultations
3. Exams and results
4. Fast search and access

### Low Priority (post-MVP)
1. Inventory
2. Scheduling
3. Prescriptions
4. Dashboard
5. Reports
6. Advanced settings

## MVP Objective
The MVP must allow a veterinary professional to:
- create and manage owners and patients
- access the complete medical record of a patient
- register a consultation using a structured workflow
- request and attach exams/results
- search quickly across patients and owners
- work in a multi-tenant environment with isolated data

## Non-Goals for MVP
The following modules should not be fully implemented in the first phase:
- full inventory management
- appointment agenda
- financial modules
- advanced analytics
- external integrations
- native mobile app

## Product Vision
The long-term goal is to evolve Vetflow Platform into a scalable SaaS product for veterinary clinics and independent professionals, with responsive web and future native mobile applications.
